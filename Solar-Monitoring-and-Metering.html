Solar Monitoring & Metering

Features:

  • Gateway: Advantech or Moxa, complete with preloaded application software and the client-specified communication drivers for the inverter, weather station, and string monitoring
  • Power Meter: Carlo Gavazzi, new Veris E series with display, or Elkor "Watts On"
  • Enclosure: metal, polycarbonate, or fiberglass
  • Terminal Blocks (fused & un-fused): mounted on raised and angled DIN rail for easier field wire connection
  • Current Transformers: large selection of both solid core and split core are available
  • CT Shorting Blocks: shorting blocks provided with all Power Meters and CTs that have a 5A secondary for safety
  • 24VDC Power Supplies: field proven 24VDC power supplies, sized per application

Whether it is our client’s design, our design, or a collaborative design, DST is committed to providing the most reliable and cost effective electrical and/or electronic sub-system products available.

DST’s Metering and Monitoring panels are manufactured in our ISO 9001/2008 registered manufacturing facility to provide our clients with the highest quality, most up to date products on the market. Our stringent procedures and methodologies result in products that exceed market standards for safety, reliability, detailed and useful documentation, and traceability. Not only do we serialize each panel but also take photos of each panel prior to shipping.

Our large manufacturing facility, located in Benicia, California, is certified to UL-508A, UL-67, UL-1741 standards and can build and document for CE compliance as well.

DST Controls also offers full service Product & System design, engineering, software development, programming, documentation, and pre-and-post sales support for monitoring & metering products and systems. So if you need it, our engineering group can design it and our manufacturing group can build it.
